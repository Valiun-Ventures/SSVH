Project Overview

The Grand Maratha is SSVH's flagship project, offering premium residential plots in one of Indore's most sought-after locations. Spread across 25 acres of lush greenery, this project combines modern amenities with traditional values.

With plot sizes ranging from 1200 to 2400 sq.ft, The Grand Maratha is perfect for building your dream home. The project features wide internal roads, landscaped gardens, 24/7 security, and all modern amenities.
