RERA Compliance

All our projects are fully compliant with RERA regulations

What is RERA?

The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (RERA) is an Act of the Parliament of India which aims to protect home-buyers and help boost investments in the real estate industry.

At SSVH, we are committed to complete transparency and legal compliance. All our projects are registered under RERA, ensuring your investment is protected by law.

Key Benefits of RERA

  • Standardized carpet area calculation
  • Protection against project delays
  • Transparency in project details
  • Escrow account for funds management
